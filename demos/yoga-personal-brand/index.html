Namaste, I'm Kanha

I hold an M.A. in Yogic Science from Jairam Mahavidyalaya, Rishikesh, and have taught for the past six years — including at Drishti Yogshala, Rishikesh. My work is therapeutic yoga: helping people aged 35 to 75 move freely, breathe deeply and live with less pain — through practice built around your body, not a template.
