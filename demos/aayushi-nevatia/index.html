Case studies

Four brands. Four problems. One approach.

01

Voylla

Fashion jewellery

Flagship

Situation

Break a multi-year follower plateau, grow Instagram engagement and turn social into a meaningful traffic channel — while building a full-year campaign ecosystem.

Strategy

Rebuilt the content and influencer engine around a structured monthly creator programme, backed by 8 end-to-end seasonal campaigns spanning digital and offline.

Execution

  • BAU influencer programme — 7–8 monthly creators, 70K–100K+ organic reach
  • 8 × 360° campaigns: Valentine's, Holi, Akshaya Tritiya, Summer, Rakhi, Mother's Day, Festive & Wedding
  • CRM across WhatsApp, SMS & email; Meta Ads content strategy
160%Instagram profile visits
95%YoY website traffic via social
117%NPS improvement
4–5 yrfollower plateau broken
02

Petcrux

Pet care

Launch

Situation

Launch new products in a crowded pet-care market through influencer-led storytelling, with conversion — not vanity metrics — as the goal.

Strategy

Influencer pilots around hero product launches, pairing creator credibility with tightly-tracked commercial funnels.

Execution

  • Launch pilots for Smart Clump & Earthy Clump cat litter
  • Brand strategy, tone of voice and social presence built from zero
  • New website build + Amazon brand store revamp in parallel
